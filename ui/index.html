The food pyramid and its parts

The food pyramid has 5 categories. The first and the bigest

is the grain category which contains rice, wheat, corn, bread, and potatoes. The second
is the fruits and vegetables category. The third is proteins which includs meat
and beeans and diary products. The last
one is oils, sugars and fats. Teh food pyramid is a basic understanding of how much
to eat from each category.
